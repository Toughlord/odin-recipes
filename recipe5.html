Chicken Parmesan Casserole

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked chicken, chopped
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup garlic croutons, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon dried Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Step 2: Spread chopped chicken in the bottom of a baking dish.
  3. Step 3: Pour marinara sauce evenly over the chicken.
  4. Step 4: Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses over the sauce.
  5. Step 5: Top with crushed garlic croutons and Italian seasoning.
  6. Step 6: Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Step 7: Serve hot and enjoy!
