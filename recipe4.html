Tomato Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 large tomatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 10 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onion
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Step 2: Place sliced tomatoes in a colander and sprinkle with salt. Let drain for 10 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Layer tomatoes, basil, and green onion in the baked pie crust. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Step 4: In a bowl, mix together mozzarella, cheddar, and mayonnaise. Spread mixture over the tomatoes.
  5. Step 5: Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until top is golden and bubbly.
  6. Step 6: Let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
