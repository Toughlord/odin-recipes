Apple Cider Hawaiian Roll Donuts

Ingredients

  • 12 Hawaiian sweet rolls
  • 1 cup apple cider
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Heat apple cider in a small saucepan over medium heat until reduced by half; set aside to cool.
  2. Step 2: In a small bowl, mix granulated sugar and cinnamon together.
  3. Step 3: In another bowl, whisk powdered sugar and milk to make a glaze.
  4. Step 4: Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  5. Step 5: Cut Hawaiian rolls in half and fry in hot oil until golden brown, about 1 minute per side.
  6. Step 6: Drain donuts on paper towels, then dip in reduced apple cider.
  7. Step 7: Coat donuts in cinnamon sugar mixture or drizzle with glaze.
  8. Step 8: Serve warm and enjoy!
