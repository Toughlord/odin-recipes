Unbelievable Chicken

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a large bowl, mix olive oil, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, parsley, basil, oregano, and black pepper.
  2. Step 2: Place chicken breasts in the bowl and turn to coat. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Preheat grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Step 4: Remove chicken from marinade and grill for 6 to 8 minutes on each side, or until juices run clear.
  5. Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy!
