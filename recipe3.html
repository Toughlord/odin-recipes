Best Taco Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet taco seasoning mix
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 1 (15 ounce) can refried beans
  • 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 (9 ounce) bag tortilla chips, crushed

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Step 2: In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned; drain excess fat.
  3. Step 3: Stir in taco seasoning and water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Spread refried beans in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  5. Step 5: Layer cooked beef mixture over beans.
  6. Step 6: Spread sour cream over beef, then pour salsa over sour cream.
  7. Step 7: Sprinkle Cheddar and Monterey Jack cheeses evenly on top.
  8. Step 8: Top with crushed tortilla chips.
  9. Step 9: Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Step 10: Serve hot and enjoy!
